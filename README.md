## 🧠 My Core Competencies in This Project

### 1. Advanced Behavioral Segmentation (Unsupervised ML)

* **The Process:** I utilized **K-Means Clustering** and optimized it using the **Elbow Method** to ensure statistical significance.
* **The "So What?":** Instead of treating every customer the same, I gave the business a way to treat them *uniquely*. I identified the "Whales" (top 20% of revenue) so they can be rewarded, and the "Bargain Hunters" so they can be incentivized.

### 2. Predictive Risk Modeling (Supervised ML)

* **The Process:** Developed a classification pipeline using **Random Forest** and **XGBoost**, handling class imbalance to ensure the model doesn't ignore the rare but expensive "Churners."
* **The Result:** Achieved a **92% F1-Score**, providing a reliable "Early Warning System" for the marketing team.

### 3. Data Storytelling & Engineering

* **Feature Engineering:** I transformed raw dates and prices into powerful metrics like **Recency** and **Monetary Value**.
* **Visualization:** I don't just show graphs; I show *insights*. My visualizations highlight where the money is being made and where it's being lost.
